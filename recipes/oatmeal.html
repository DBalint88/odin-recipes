Oatmeal

Description:

This delicious and healthy breakfast goes together as easy as it goes down.

Get two of your five-a-day right off the start line with this hardy bowl of banana oatmeal topped with your choice of fresh or frozen blueberries. Anti-oxidants, vitamins, fiber - it's a real keeper!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Trader Joe's Ancient Oats
  • 1 cup water
  • One (1) fresh banana
  • One cup fresh or frozen blueberries

Steps:

  1. Measure out the 1/2 cup of oats and pour them into your bowl.
  2. Measure out the 1 cup of water and pour it into the pot. Boil over high heat.
  3. Once the water is boiling, slice the banana into the boiling water.
  4. As soon as the banana slices start coming apart, add the oatmeal to the water.
  5. Stir vigilantly for one minute or until the desired consistency is achieved, being careful not to let any oatmeal stick to the bottom (or burn!)
  6. Pour the cooked oatmeal back into the bowl and top with the blueberries.
