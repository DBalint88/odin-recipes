Sweet Potato Fries

Description:

Salty, spicy, and savory, these healthy(-er) french fry alternatives will fill you up, and never let you down.

Why settle for plain white shoe-string fries? Why eat the over-processed, preservative laden excuses for fries you get in the frozen aisle? Take a visit to the produce aisle and lets make a side dish you can be proud of.

Ingredients:

  • 1 fresh sweet potato
  • 1 tbsp peanut oil
  • Seasonings: Salt, pepper, paprika, garlic and onion powders

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 450F with a cast iron roasting tray inside.
  2. Wash the sweet potato.
  3. Slice the sweet potato into ~1cm wide coins, then slice those coins the other way to make 1cm x 1cm sticks of varying length
  4. Once the oven is pre-heated, remove the roasting tray and add to it the fries.
  5. Pour onto the fries the oil and stir the fries until they are evenly coated.
  6. Repeat the last step with the seasonings.
  7. Roast at 450 for 10 minutes. Remove and stir, then roast for 10 more minutes.
