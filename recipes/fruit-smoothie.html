Fruit Smoothie

Description:

Nothing is more refreshing than a glass of sweet, cold, fresh fruit smoothie!

Enjoy a sweet and tangy treat that's relatively guilt free and guaranteed to cool you off on a hot summer day. As a matter of fact, it'll cool you off any day, year-round! ...if that's what you're into.

Ingredients:

  • One fresh banana
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 cup frozen raspberries
  • One lime, squeezed
  • One cup soy milk

Steps:

  1. Add the banana, blueberries, and raspberries to the blender.
  2. Pour in the soy milk and squeeze completely one lime into the mixture
  3. Blend until smooth and serve in glass!
